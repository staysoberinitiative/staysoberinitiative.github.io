About Us

The Stay Sober Initiative CBO was founded in October 2023 with a steadfast commitment to actively engaging and assisting the community in comprehending the intricacies of alcoholism. Our primary focus revolves around aiding individuals grappling with alcohol and drug use disorders, guiding them along the path toward sobriety.

Our Vision

To walk with drug and alcohol use disorder towards sobriety.

Our Mission

To be a community-based organization that holds hands with persons with substance used disorder in our endeavor to help them towards sobriety.

Our Objectives

  • To assist people with substance, use disorder.
  • To offer intervention, assessment and treatment services.
  • To offer rehabilitation services and outpatient services.
  • To organize community outreach services.
  • To work towards forming a social enterprise for sustainability.
  • To organize seminars conventions and workshops
  • To create a conducive and healthy environment that is suitable to the community
